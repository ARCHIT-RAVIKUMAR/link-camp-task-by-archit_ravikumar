About the Event

What is IEEE LINK?

IEEE Local Integrated Network of Kerala (LINK) is the vibrant student activities board of the IEEE Kerala Section, connecting student branches across the state to foster leadership and networking.

About LINK CAMP

The annual flagship event of IEEE LINK. It is an exclusive 3-day leadership camp where selected delegates from across Kerala gather for strategic planning, skill development, and networking.

Event Timeline

Day 1 - Jan 24

Inauguration & Networking

Official flag-off, ice-breaking sessions, and team formation for delegates.

Day 2 - Jan 25

Leadership Training

Interactive sessions on volunteering, organizational management, and strategic planning.

Day 3 - Jan 26

Valedictory Ceremony

Closing remarks, awards distribution, and photo sessions.

Selection Process

LINK CAMP is an invitation-only event. Delegates are selected through a rigorous 3-stage process.

01

Task Completion

Applicants must complete technical or managerial tasks assigned by the team.

02

Interview

Shortlisted candidates undergo a personal interview to assess leadership potential.

03

Final Selection

The top candidates are invited to attend LINK CAMP 2025 as official delegates.
