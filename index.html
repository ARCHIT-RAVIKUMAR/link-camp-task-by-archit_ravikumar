About the Event

What is IEEE LINK?

IEEE Local Integrated Network of Kerala (LINK) is the vibrant student activities board of the IEEE Kerala Section. We connect student branches across the state to foster technology and leadership.

About LINK CAMP

LINK CAMP is our annual flagship gathering. It brings together student leaders, innovators, and professionals for 3 days of workshops, networking, and strategic planning for the year ahead.
